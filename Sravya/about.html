Why Bookmap?


Distraction-free interface

The Internet is all about being distracted. Reading is all about being focused.
That’s why we reduced the number of elements on a site.
You won’t see any Google ads on our site, that’s a promise.


Simple tips for BookMap lovers

Many users still think bookmap is too complicated.
What they need is dead-simple tips on bookmap, e-readers, tablets, any piece of technology which is needed to start reading bookmap.
Or they just simply want to be persuaded to try bookmap.
Tips, more section is devoted to answer these needs.


Helping international bookmapusers

Most bookmapsites focus on US market, but bookmap is taking off in many countries.
There is no point in sharing US-only news for users in other countries without adding a special context and perspective. We fill this gap.
